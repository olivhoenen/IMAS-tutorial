# Welcome to the IMAS (Integrated Modelling and Analysis Suite) Tutorial!

This series of notebooks will introduce you to IMAS and various of its software components. 

This tutorial is designed for newcomers to IMAS who should follow topics in order. This material can also allow be useful for long-time users who may want to refresh their knowledge or jump directly to a topic and discover the associated recommended tools.

This tutorial can be followed online, just click on the Colab badge above. 
For getting deeper on any of the software covered in this tutorial, please use its respective documentation and dedicated training material if it exists.
